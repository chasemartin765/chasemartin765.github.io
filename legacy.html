Legacy

Steph Curry has had a lasting impact on the league, and has changed the game forever. He has used his long-distance shooting to his advantage, leading to changes in the way teams defend, and how they implemented their offensives. He has also influenced many young players by way of his flashy shooting and handling of the ball, leading to the rise of new young players with similar play styles as him.

He has the fastest jumpshot ever recorded
